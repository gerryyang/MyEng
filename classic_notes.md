
记录的一些经典表达
---

* I used to know some French, but now it has found its way back to my teacher.

我过去学过些法语的，不过现在全还给老师了。

* A: May I have your attention please? B: I'm all ears!

我洗耳恭听。(选自《CSDN论坛》)

* A nurse should have patience in her work.

当护士要有耐心。

* As a result of timely rains, the food production was plentiful in that area this year.

由于雨水及时，今年该地区粮食产量充足。

* When we learn to treasure simple happiness then we will be winners in life.

当我们懂得珍惜平凡的幸福时就已经成了人生的赢家。(选自《筷子兄弟》)

* Let's forgive and forget.

让我们摒弃前嫌。

* The American team definitely caught a break with that goal.

美国队的那粒进球根本就是走了狗屎运啊。

* To know more colloquial expressions is a head start in learning English.

了解更多的俗语对学习英语有好处。

* Guan yunchang went to feast by himself.

单刀赴会。(选自 西安鼓楼)

* Do what you love and fuck the rest.

做你喜欢的事，其他都尼玛的扯蛋。(选自 腾讯微博)

* It is a long story.

一言难尽。

* Between us.

你知，我知。

* Come on, be reasonable.

嗨，你怎么不讲道理。

* When are you leaving?

你什么时候走？

* Don't get me wrong.

别误会我。

* It's up to you.

由你决定。

* The line is engaged.

占线。

* My hands are full right now.

我现在很忙。

* Can you dig it?

你搞明白了吗？

* How big of you!
你真棒！

* Who wants?

谁稀罕？

* Follow my nose.

凭直觉做某事。

* Cheap skate!

小气鬼！

* Dinner is on me.

晚饭我请。

* You ask for it!

活该！

* Don't mention it.

没关系，别客气。

* It is not a big deal!

没什么了不起！

* Who knows!

天晓得！

* Don't let me down.

别让我失望。

* It doesn't take much of your time.

这不花你好多时间。
